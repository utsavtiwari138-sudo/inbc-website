Past Event Highlights

🏟️

Sammelan 2025 — Bhopal

Over 5,000 attendees from 18 states gathered for three days of deliberations, cultural programs, and resolutions on community development. Key resolutions on education and youth were passed.

📜

Sanskrit Diwas Celebration

National-level Sanskrit Day celebration in Varanasi with scholars, poets, and students. 200+ participants engaged in debates, poetry recitals, and Vedic chanting competitions.

🏥

Mega Health Camp — Patna

Free medical camp providing health check-ups to 3,000+ community members. Eye care, dental, cardiac screening, and Ayurvedic consultations were offered at no cost.

💍

Matrimonial Meet — Jaipur

Successfully facilitated 150+ matches at the annual matrimonial meet. Families from Rajasthan, UP, MP, and Gujarat participated in the highly organized event.

👨‍🎓

Career Guidance Workshop

2-day career guidance workshop for 500+ young Brahmans with sessions by IAS officers, doctors, entrepreneurs, and IT professionals from the community.

🎭

Rangmanch — Cultural Festival

Three-day cultural extravaganza featuring classical music, dance, drama, and literary events. Artists from 12 states showcased the community's rich artistic heritage.
